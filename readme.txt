# Harvey Specter AI Trader

Welcome to the Harvey Specter AI Trader - the epitome of sharp wit, confidence, and unmatched trading acumen. This isn't just another trading tool; it's a statement, a lifestyle, a game-changer. Crafted with the finesse and charisma reminiscent of New York City's top closer, this AI trader is designed to give you the edge you've always sought in the stock market.

## Features:

1. **Real-time Stock Data**: Leveraging the power of Google Search, get up-to-the-minute stock prices.
2. **News Headlines**: Stay informed with the latest news headlines related to your stock, fetched in real-time.
3. **Harvey Specter Persona**: Get trading advice with the confidence and flair only Harvey Specter can provide, powered by ChatGPT.
4. **Advanced Stock Analysis**: Utilize state-of-the-art algorithms like Random Forests, ARIMA, and SVR for predictive analysis.
5. **Sleek GUI**: A professional Wall Street look and feel, because style matters.

## Setup:

1. **Dependencies**: Ensure you have all the necessary libraries installed. Refer to `requirements.txt`.
2. **API Keys**: Set up your Google Search API and OpenAI API keys in the `config_final.py` file.
3. **Run**: Execute the `main_app_complete.py` to launch the application.

## How to Use:

1. **Enter Stock Name**: Input the name or ticker of the stock you're interested in.
2. **Get Advice**: Click the 'Get Advice' button and let Harvey do his magic. You'll receive a stock analysis, price prediction, and a piece of classic Harvey Specter advice.
3. **Stock Score**: Based on advanced algorithms, get a score between 1-100 indicating the stock's potential. The score is visually represented in a gradient from red (low) to green (high).
